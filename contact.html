Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting us and our programs

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your message.

What information should I include in my message?

Please provide your full name, email, and a clear description of your inquiry. If you're interested in a specific program, mention that as well. This helps us respond more effectively.

Can I schedule a call with an advisor?

Absolutely! Once you submit your inquiry, our team will follow up with you to schedule a convenient time for a consultation call.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations to discuss your pre-medical goals and how our programs can help you succeed in your medical school journey.

What if I have technical issues with the form?

If you experience any technical difficulties, please email us directly at premedpoland@gmail.com with your inquiry, and we'll be happy to assist you.
